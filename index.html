Research profile

Cloud physics, resolved particle by particle.

My work sits at the intersection of cloud microphysics, turbulence, and computational science. I develop particle-based methods for SCALE-SDM and use high-resolution simulations to understand entrainment-mixing, precipitation initiation, and the lifecycle of individual hydrometeors.

01Lagrangian particle-based microphysicsSuper-Droplet Method development, numerical convergence, and cloud microphysics parameterization.

02Turbulence–microphysics interactionsLarge-eddy and direct numerical simulations of entrainment, mixing, and precipitation initiation.

03Lagrangian particle trackingForward and backward Lagrangian tracking for reconstructing complete hydrometeor life histories.

04Scalable cloud simulationHigh-performance computing with SCALE-SDM, MPI, OpenMP, Fortran, and Python workflows.
